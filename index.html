Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the moon sometimes out during the day? +
The moon is sometimes visible during the day because it is reflecting sunlight, just like it does at night.
Why is the sky blue? +
The sky is blue because molecules in the air scatter blue light from the sun more than they scatter red light.
Will we ever discover aliens? +
It's possible, but we have not found any definitive evidence of extraterrestrial life yet.
How much does the Earth weigh? +
The Earth weighs about 5.972 10^24 kilograms.
How do airplanes stay up? +
Airplanes stay up because of the lift generated by their wings as they move through the air.
